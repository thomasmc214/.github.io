Becoming a self-taught developer

My name is Tom, and this is my story about how (at the age of 56) I decided to become a self-taught developer.

My story begins roughly a year and a half ago. I have been self-employed for the past 4 to 5 years as a delivery driver working in the "gig-economy". One day I came to the realization, that while being self-employed is definitely the way to go (at least,for me),when you are working as a driver there can be lots of hidden expenses through wear and tear on ones vehicle.
